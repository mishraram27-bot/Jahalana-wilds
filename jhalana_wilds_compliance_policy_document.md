# Jhalana Wilds: Legal & Compliance Framework (V1.0)

## 1. Terms & Conditions of Service
*   **Booking Authority:** All safaris are operated and regulated by the Rajasthan Forest Department. Jhalana Wilds acts as a specialized facilitation and conservation partner.
*   **Permit Validity:** Permits are non-transferable and valid only for the specific date, time slot, and zone mentioned.
*   **Code of Conduct:** Guests must adhere to "Ethical Safari" protocols. Any behavior distressing wildlife or violating forest department rules will result in immediate expedition termination without refund.
*   **Liability:** Jhalana Wilds is not liable for wildlife sightings (which are natural occurrences) or administrative park closures by the Government of Rajasthan.

## 2. Cancellation & Refund Policy
*   **Standard Cancellation:**
    *   30+ Days Before: 75% Refund of the service fee (Permit fees are government-regulated and usually non-refundable).
    *   15-29 Days Before: 50% Refund of the service fee.
    *   <15 Days: 0% Refund.
*   **Weather/Force Majeure:** In the event of park closure due to extreme weather or government mandates, guests will be offered a priority rescheduling window or a 100% credit for the next 12 months.

## 3. Privacy & Data Consent
*   **Citizen Scientist Data:** By using the Field Companion app, guests consent to the anonymous use of their sighting data and photos for conservation research.
*   **Media Rights:** Participation in a "Photography Masterclass" implies consent for Jhalana Wilds to use behind-the-scenes footage for marketing, unless a "Private/NDA" package is selected.
*   **Data Protection:** We comply with global data protection standards (GDPR/DPDP); guest personal data is never sold to third-party travel aggregators.

## 4. Ethical Safari Consent (The "Citizen Scientist" Pledge)
*   **Proximity Rules:** I agree to maintain the mandatory 20-meter distance from felines.
*   **Silence Protocol:** I agree to maintain absolute silence and disable camera flashes during sightings.
*   **Plastic-Free Zone:** I commit to carrying zero single-use plastics into the reserve.
