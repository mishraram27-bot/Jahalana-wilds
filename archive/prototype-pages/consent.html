emergency EMERGENCY SOS policy FOREST RULES
By booking a safari with Jhalana Wilds, you agree to comply with all Rajasthan Forest Department regulations. This includes maintaining silence within the reserve, prohibiting littering, and following guide instructions without question.
Your sighting data and location are used exclusively to improve conservation efforts. We never share your personal identification with third parties without your explicit "Citizen Scientist" consent.
Cancellations made 72 hours before the scheduled safari are eligible for a 90% refund. Within 72 hours, no refunds are issued due to fixed government permit costs.
