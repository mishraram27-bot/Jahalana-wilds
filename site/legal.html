1. Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: October 24, 2024

1.1 Scope of Service

Jhalana Wilds provides high-end expedition and safari services within the Jhalana Leopard Reserve. By booking a safari, you agree to comply with the Forest Department of Rajasthan regulations and our internal safety protocols.

1.2 Booking & Eligibility

All participants must be at least 6 years of age. Safaris are booked on a first-come, first-served basis through our secure portal. Jhalana Wilds reserves the right to refuse service to any individual who violates safety protocols during prior visits.

1.3 Wilderness Risks

Wildlife encounters are unpredictable. While Jhalana Wilds takes every precaution to ensure safety, participants acknowledge the inherent risks of entering a natural habitat. Liability is limited to the extent permitted by Indian Law.

Safari sightings are natural and cannot be guaranteed. Booking assistance is subject to availability, permit rules, vehicle allocation, and final confirmation over phone or WhatsApp.

1.4 Code of Conduct

Noise pollution, littering, and any attempt to feed or provoke animals will result in immediate expulsion from the park without a refund. Our guides have the final authority on all field decisions.

"We preserve the wild for those who follow. Your compliance ensures the leopard's legacy remains untainted by human footprint."
