# Manual Booking Flow

## Current Business Flow

1. Visitor reviews safari information, packages, gallery, policies, or photography options.
2. Visitor starts an enquiry through WhatsApp, phone, or a Netlify form.
3. Business team confirms requirements manually.
4. Business team discusses date preference, group size, pickup/drop needs, visitor nationality, photography needs, and any special assistance.
5. Business team checks practical feasibility and forest entry requirements outside the website.
6. Business team confirms next steps directly with the customer by phone or WhatsApp.

## What the Website Must Not Claim

- No instant confirmation
- No live slot availability
- No online checkout
- No automated permit booking
- No customer login
- No dashboard access
- No generated certificate
- No live or verified field-data tracker
