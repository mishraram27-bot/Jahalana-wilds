# Jhalana Wilds: End-to-End Guest Journey Walkthrough

## Phase 1: Discovery & Aspiration
- **Homepage:** Guest arrives at the high-conversion landing page. They are immediately greeted by the "Live Sightings Radar" showing recent leopard activity, establishing trust and "FOMO."
- **Photography Masterclass:** For the elite segment, they explore the Masterclass page, viewing the "Members Only" gallery of past workshop results.

## Phase 2: Booking & Preparation
- **Expedition Selection:** Guest chooses between "Luxe Photography" or "Citizen Scientist" packages.
- **Secure Checkout:** Transparent pricing and "Ethical Safari" badges guide them through a secure booking flow.
- **Permit Receipt:** Guest receives their "Digital Safari Permit" on mobile, with arrival instructions and their naturalist's profile.

## Phase 3: The Safari Experience (The "Citizen Scientist" Loop)
- **Field Companion App:** Upon entry, guests use the app in Dark Mode to track their location and view the historical sightings log.
- **AI Species ID:** When a leopard is spotted, the guest uses the AI tool to identify the individual (e.g., "Rana") via rosette matching.
- **Naturalist Interaction:** The naturalist logs the sighting via the Driver Portal, which enters the Naturalist Verification Queue.

## Phase 4: Fulfillment & Community
- **Certification:** After the safari, the guest receives a premium "Citizen Scientist Certificate" populated with the specific leopards they identified.
- **Sustainability Dashboard:** Guest reviews the conservation impact of their trip (hectares protected, local community support).
- **Post-Safari Gallery:** Guests are invited to upload their best shots to the Members Gallery, keeping them connected to the Jhalana Wilds community.

## Phase 5: Operational Excellence (Back-End)
- **Client Admin Dashboard:** The owner monitors site analytics, booking trends, and sighting success rates.
- **Media Library:** Management uploads authentic new field footage to keep the site content fresh and authoritative.
