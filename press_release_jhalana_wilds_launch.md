# Jhalana Wilds Unveils "Citizen Scientist" Ecosystem: A New Era of Transparent, Ethical Leopard Safaris in Jaipur

**JAIPUR, RAJASTHAN** – Jhalana Wilds, Jaipur’s premier private safari operator, today announced the launch of its revolutionary "Citizen Scientist" digital ecosystem. This integrated platform sets a new global benchmark for transparency, ethical wildlife viewing, and guest engagement in urban leopard conservation.

At the heart of the launch is a real-time "Live Sightings Radar" and an interactive field companion app that allows guests to participate directly in conservation efforts. By logging sightings and identifying individual leopards like the famous "Rana" and "Flora," guests transition from passive observers to active contributors to the reserve’s population database.

"Conservation in the 21st century is no longer just about boundaries and guards; it is about radical transparency and community integration," said Rajesh V. Singh, Head of Conservation at Jhalana Wilds. "By providing a premium, tech-enabled experience, we generate the capital necessary to deploy military-grade surveillance and fund sustainable healthcare for the 14 villages bordering the park."

### Key Features of the Jhalana Wilds Ecosystem:
*   **Live Sighting Radar:** Real-time data feeds from field naturalists providing unprecedented transparency on leopard movement and sighting probabilities.
*   **AI Species Identification:** A mobile-first AR interface for guests to identify individual leopards in the field based on unique rosette patterns.
*   **Ethical Viewing Protocols:** Rigorous digital tracking of gypsy proximity to ensure zero disturbance to feline behavior.
*   **Citizen Scientist Certification:** Every guest receives a verified digital and physical record of their contribution to the Jhalana Leopard Database.

Designed for the elite photography and eco-tourism market, Jhalana Wilds offers specialized "Master the Wild" workshops and private expeditions that prioritize animal welfare over "sightseeing at any cost."

The 2026 Season is now open for early bird bookings, featuring a new "Season Forecast" interactive map that predicts high-activity corridors based on five years of historical field data.

### About Jhalana Wilds
Jhalana Wilds is a conservation-first safari operator dedicated to the protection of urban leopards in the Aravalli hills. Combining luxury hospitality with rigorous field science, they provide world-class wildlife encounters that fund critical habitat corridor expansion and anti-poaching technology.
