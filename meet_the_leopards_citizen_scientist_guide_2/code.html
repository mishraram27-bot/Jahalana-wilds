Meet the Residents

Each Jhalana leopard has a distinct identity, personality, and territory. Use our field guide to recognize them during your expedition.

workspace_premium Citizen Scientist Verified

Flora

"The Queen of Zone 1"

Sighting Frequency Very High

Photography Tip

Flora is active in low brush; use a 70-200mm for dynamic movement shots.

Known for her exceptionally calm demeanor around vehicles, Flora has raised three litters in the central plateau area.

Rana

"The Aravali King"

Territory Size 24 sq km

Photography Tip

Rana prefers high rocky outcrops; a 600mm lens is recommended for distance.

The dominant male of the southern cliffs. Rana is famous for his deep, guttural growl that can be heard across the valley.

Juliet

"The Photogenic Muse"

Last Seen 4 Hours Ago

Photography Tip

Juliet is the photogenic muse; look for her near waterholes at dusk.

A favorite among photographers, Juliet often poses atop the rocky outcrops near the waterholes of Amagarh.
