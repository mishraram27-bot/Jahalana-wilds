____ How many leopards are there in Jhalana Jaipur?

Jhalana Leopard Conservation Reserve has more than 40 Leopards including 5 cubs. The last tiger was shot in 1948. Jhalana is spread over an area of 20.

____ What animals are in the Jhalana leopard safari?

Apart from the leopard, striped hyenas also call Jhalana home. Ungulates like blue bulls, spotted deer and some sambhar deer are also present. Desert fox, jackals, rusty spotted cat, desert cat and jungle cats can also be seen. Porcupines, monitor lizards, mongoose and lots of varieties of snakes are in abundance here.

____ Which is better Jhalana or Amagarh?

Overall, Jhalana is a better choice for visitors who are looking for the best possible chance of seeing a leopard and who are comfortable with a more traditional safari experience.

____ Which zone is better in Jhalana?

All three zones in Jhalana Leopard Reserve are equally good for spotting leopards and other wildlife. The terrain in each zone is different, but all zones offer a variety of habitats that leopards and other animals favor.
