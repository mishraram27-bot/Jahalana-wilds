# Social Media Launch Kit: Jhalana Wilds "Citizen Scientist" Campaign

## Visual Direction
- **Primary Colors:** Deep Forest Green (#064e3b), Aravali Sand (from Design System).
- **Typography:** Playfair Display for headlines, clean Sans-Serif for body.
- **Mood:** Educational, premium, emotionally resonant.

## Instagram Story Templates
1. **Live Sighting Alert:** Dark green overlay on a leopard photo with a "LIVE NOW" sticker, zone location, and a "Swipe Up to Book" call-to-action.
2. **Meet the Leopard:** Spotlight on "Flora" or "Rana" with three key facts (Territory, Personality, Sighting Odds).
3. **Citizen Scientist Spotlight:** Reposting a guest's photo with the "Verified Sighting" badge.

## Instagram/Facebook Grid Posts
1. **The "Why" Carousel:**
   - Slide 1: "More than just a safari." (Hero shot of a leopard)
   - Slide 2: "Every photo you take helps us protect them." (Shot of a naturalist with a tablet)
   - Slide 3: "Join the Citizen Scientist movement." (Shot of the digital certificate)
2. **The "Live Radar" Proof:** A screenshot of the Live Radar showing recent movement, captioned "The numbers don't lie. 90% sighting success this week."

## Call-to-Action (CTA) Strategy
- Use: "Claim your spot in the tracking Gypsy."
- Use: "Become a Citizen Scientist."
- Use: "Track the ghosts of Jhalana."
