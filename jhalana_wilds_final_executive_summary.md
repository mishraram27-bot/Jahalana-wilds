# Jhalana Wilds: Final Executive Summary
## Strategic Design & Operational Documentation

**Project Name:** Jhalana Wilds
**Brand Identity:** Wilderness Luxe
**Core Narrative:** The Citizen Scientist Ecosystem

---

### 1. Brand Identity & Design Tokens

The "Wilderness Luxe" system is designed to bridge the gap between high-end hospitality and rigorous field science.

#### **Color Palette**
*   **Deep Forest Green (#064e3b):** The primary anchor. Represents conservation, depth, and the Jhalana canopy.
*   **Sunset Amber:** Used for calls-to-action (CTAs) and live sighting markers to evoke the golden hour.
*   **Aravali Sand:** A neutral, warm surface tone derived from the rocky terrain of the Aravalli hills.
*   **Ink Black:** Used for Dark Mode field tools (Driver Portal, Field Companion) to reduce glare during dawn/dusk operations.

#### **Typography**
*   **Headlines:** *Playfair Display* (Serif). Conveys heritage, authority, and premium positioning.
*   **Body & UI:** *Clean Sans-Serif* (Inter/System). Ensures high legibility for data-heavy interfaces and field tools.

#### **Visual Language**
*   **Roundness:** `ROUND_FOUR` (Subtle 4px radius) for a professional, structured feel.
*   **Imagery:** High-contrast wildlife photography with deep shadows and warm highlights.

---

### 2. Component Logic & Shared Patterns

#### **Navigation Framework**
*   **Desktop (TopNavBar):** Features "Leopards," "Zones," "Packages," and "Ethical Safari." The primary action is always "Check Availability."
*   **Mobile (BottomNavBar):** A high-utility 4-tab bar:
    1.  **Explore:** General browsing.
    2.  **Live Tracker:** Real-time sightings radar.
    3.  **My Bookings:** Permit and certificate management.
    4.  **Support:** SOS and naturalist assistance.

#### **The "Citizen Scientist" Data Loop**
1.  **Input (Field):** Drivers and Naturalists log sightings via the **Driver Portal** (Dark Mode).
2.  **Verification:** Data is synchronized and verified in the **Naturalist Academy** portal.
3.  **Visualization (Public):** The **Homepage Live Radar** and **Sightings Log** auto-update with verified data.
4.  **Fulfillment:** Guests receive a **Digital Certificate** populated with the specific leopards they identified during their expedition.
