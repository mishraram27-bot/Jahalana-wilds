# Jhalana Wilds: 3-Month Marketing Roadmap

## Month 1: The "Grand Reveal" (Awareness)
- **Week 1:** Press Release distribution to travel trade publications. 
- **Week 2:** Launch the "Meet the Leopards" social media series (Instagram/Facebook) featuring profiles of Rana and Flora.
- **Week 3:** Influencer "Sneak Peek" expeditions for top wildlife photographers.
- **Week 4:** "Citizen Scientist" Campaign Launch: Educational content explaining how guest data helps conservation.

## Month 2: The "Live Edge" (Engagement)
- **Week 5-6:** Daily "Morning Sightings" updates on Instagram Stories using the Live Radar screenshots.
- **Week 7:** Launch the "Season Forecast" interactive map for early bird 2026 bookings.
- **Week 8:** Spotlight on the Sustainability Dashboard: "Your Safari, Their Survival" series.

## Month 3: Conversion & Community (Action)
- **Week 9:** Release the "Photography Masterclass" series: Behind-the-scenes footage from elite workshops.
- **Week 10:** Guest Spotlight Month: Sharing "Citizen Scientist" certificates and guest-taken photos from the Members Gallery.
- **Week 11:** "Last Call" for the 2026 Season Forecast discounts.
- **Week 12:** Anniversary Report: Sharing the first quarter's impact on habitat protection and community health.

## Core Content Pillars
1. **Authenticity:** Real sightings, real data, no staged photography.
2. **Exclusivity:** Positioning Jhalana Wilds as the premium, tech-enabled gateway to the reserve.
3. **Purpose:** Every booking is a conservation act.
