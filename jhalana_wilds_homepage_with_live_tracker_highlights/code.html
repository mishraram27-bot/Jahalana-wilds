Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best timings for Jhalana Leopard Safari? expand_more

Early morning (6:00 AM - 9:30 AM) and late afternoon (3:30 PM - 7:00 PM) are the best times. Leopards are crepuscular, meaning they are most active during dawn and dusk.

Is it safe for children and families? expand_more

Yes, Jhalana safaris are extremely safe. We use specialized open-top gypsies, and leopards in Jhalana are accustomed to vehicle presence. However, absolute silence is required within the park.

How much does a private safari cost? expand_more

A private gypsy for Indian nationals starts at ₹14,500. This includes permits, vehicle rent, and a mandatory guide. Prices for foreign nationals differ due to government permit fees.
