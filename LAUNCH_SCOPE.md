# Launch Scope

## Approved Launch Type

This project is a static lead-generation website for Jhalana Leopard Safari enquiries.

The website helps visitors learn about safari options and contact the business. It does not complete bookings online.

## In Scope

- Jhalana Leopard Safari overview
- Safari packages and manual enquiry
- Wildlife photography safari or masterclass enquiry
- Group and corporate safari enquiry
- Pickup/drop and planning assistance copy where relevant
- WhatsApp enquiry
- Phone enquiry
- Netlify enquiry forms
- FAQ, policies, gallery, about, and trust content

## Out of Scope

- Dynamic booking system
- Real-time slot availability
- Online payment checkout
- Customer account or login
- Driver, admin, naturalist, or analytics dashboard
- Certificate generation
- Live sightings tracker
- AI species identification
- Automated forest permit booking

- `site/thank-you.html`

## Public CTA Rules

Public CTAs must only go to:

- WhatsApp
- Phone call
- A Netlify enquiry form
- Public information pages

No public CTA should send visitors to archived prototype or internal pages.
